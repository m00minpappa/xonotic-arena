Print & Play kit

// everything you need to build the game on your kitchen table

How to print

  1. US Letter, portrait, 100% / actual size (do not "fit to page" — it breaks card sizing).
  2. Color on, with "background graphics" enabled.
  3. Cardstock (200–300gsm) if you have it; printer paper works if you sleeve.
  4. Hit ⎙ Print above, or Ctrl/⌘+P.

What prints

  • P.1–2 Weapon cards (16)
  • P.3 Tech & Item cards (16)
  • P.4–5 Avatar cards (10)
  • P.6 Location tiles (8)
  • P.7 Tokens & markers
  • P.8 Fighter mats (2)

How many copies

  • Cards are 60×86mm — cut on the borders.
  • A 40-card deck allows ≤3 of any card, ≤2 per powerup. Photocopy the weapon/tech/item sheets 2–3× to build a deck.
  • Print one Fighter mat per player (2–6) and cut out one Avatar each.
  • Arrange Location tiles in a line (duel) or ring (FFA).
